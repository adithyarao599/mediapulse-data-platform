# KPI Dictionary

## Total Revenue

Definition:

Total revenue generated across all content.

Formula:

SUM(revenue)

Business Owner:

Finance Team

---

## Total Views

Definition:

Total views across all platforms.

Formula:

SUM(views)

Business Owner:

Content Team

---

## Total Streams

Definition:

Total streams across all music content.

Formula:

SUM(streams)

Business Owner:

Product Team

---

## Average Engagement Score

Definition:

Average engagement score across all content.

Formula:

AVG(engagement_score)

Business Owner:

Analytics Team

---

## Top Artist

Definition:

Artist with highest streams.

Formula:

MAX(streams)

Business Owner:

Content Team

---

## Top Genre

Definition:

Genre with highest total engagement.

Formula:

SUM(engagement_score)

## Artist Revenue

Definition:

Total revenue generated by an artist.

Formula:

SUM(revenue)

---

## Artist Streams

Definition:

Total streams generated by an artist.

Formula:

SUM(streams)

---

## Artist Engagement

Definition:

Average engagement score for artist content.

Formula:

AVG(engagement_score)

## Genre Revenue

Definition:

Revenue generated by a genre.

Formula:

SUM(revenue)

---

## Genre Streams

Definition:

Streams generated by a genre.

Formula:

SUM(streams)

## Country Views

Definition:

Total content views by country.

Formula:

SUM(views)

---

## Country Revenue

Definition:

Revenue generated by country.

Formula:

SUM(revenue)

## RPM

Definition:

Revenue Per Thousand Views.

Formula:

(revenue / views) * 1000

Business Owner:

Finance Team

## Recommendation Score

Definition:

Combined score used by recommendation engine.

Formula:

0.6 * engagement_score

*

0.4 * trend_score
